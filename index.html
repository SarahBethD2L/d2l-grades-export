Desire2Learn Grades Export

This utility lets you export grades from your Desire2Learn courses for submission in PeopleSoft.

  1. Select a course and grade item

    Choose the course and grade item that you would like to export. The grade values must be letter grades to be accepted by PeopleSoft.

  2. Download grades

    The chosen grade item will be downloaded as a file to your computer.

  3. Import into PeopleSoft

    Visit PeopleSoft to upload the grades for the chosen course. See FInal Grades & Student Administration System for assistance.
